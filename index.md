I am the **CEO of [VAIS](https://vais.vn)**, a Vietnam-based AI startup (founded 2018) that builds core AI engines with a focus on **Vietnamese speech recognition**. I received my **Master’s and PhD** from the [Augmented Human Communication Laboratory](http://ahclab.naist.jp/index_en.html) at **Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST)**, Japan.

My work focuses on **AI/ML for speech and language**: speech recognition, speech translation, document intelligence, and related topics. I have published at venues including **Interspeech**, **EMNLP**, **ICASSP**, **LREC**, **IEEE Transactions on Audio, Speech and Language Processing**, and **O-COCOSDA**.
